#ABOUT ME


A childhood dream

It has always been my dream to become an artist, I would go down the street and see amazing artworks done by the others, and one day I would like to become those who inspired me


It Began in High School

I've started drawing at the start (probably) of my secondary school (high school) life, I would draw on paper and I remembr that I watched DrawABox tutorials.

Getting Into 3D Modelling

Eventually, I realized that DrawABox isn't what I am going after. And after I've gotton into secondary 3 (10th Grade), my IT teacher would convince me into trying Blender, and I did

The First Serious Project

I remembered the very first model I made while I was figuring out the controls was a very simple sniper rifle. Eventually, I've made my first artwork and posted it onto Artstation, and that artwork was posted back in 2019!

The first work!

The Year When I got into Illustrating

In the year 2021, I got into digital painting, a fancy way of saying that I got into anime illustration

A Massive Clusterfuck™

In the very same year, The COVID pandemic broke out, which was also my year to have the public exam (HKDSE). So, you can imagine that year turned into a fucking dramma.

An Escape

Maybe because of that, making anime illustration became a form of escape, and I made quite a few works around that time.


Massive Improvements

I've came a long way since I've drawn my first illustration; And hell, its a huge difference compared to then.

My Family

Although some may say that I am somewhat talented, my family seems to see the otherwise. I don't really get support from them, whether it be emotional or financial.

Tbf they make my life quite hard, at least emotionally, but that is out of my control.

Looking Forward

But they ain't gonna point no fingers in MY life, I know the pursue in art is my destiny, I will die doing art. I swore an oath that I will never give up on pursuing art.
